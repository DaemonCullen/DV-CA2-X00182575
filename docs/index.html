Education

Primary School

Gaelscoil Faithleann was where I went to primary school as a kid and it is located in the centre of Kerry. It is a small irish speaking school in the town of Killarney and has been open since 1989 as a result of a joint working group of local parents and members of Conradh na Gaeilge. The schools logo is a branch with three sloes that represent the three lakes in Killarney. The uniform is purple to represent the colour of the sloe fruit.

Secondary School

Adamstown Community College (ACC) is where I went to school when I moved back to Dublin. It is located on Finnstown, Lucan, directly beside the train station and has over a thousand students. In ACC I took many different classes such as metalwork, technology, biology and of course computer science.

College

Technological University Dublin Tallaght or TUD Tallaght is the college that I currently attend. TUD have five different campuses in Dublin and over twenty seven thousand students. I am studing computer science and I am majoring in software development.

Final Project

Over the years of computer programming I have picked up a couple different programming languages. First is HTML/CSS which is one of my favourites as it let's you create art as well as software. Because of my love of these languages I have also picked up a little Javascript to make those website a little more interactive and alive. Another big language for me is Python as it was the first language I learned and I used it in my secondary school so it is one I am well acquainted with. Another language I love is C++/C# because growing up loving games the C languages were always ones I liked because of what they have created. Throughout my life wheater it was working on projects with people, making something alone or just doing my own thing I have deffinitly picked up my fair share of usefull soft skills along with my technical skills. One easy one is Creativity which comes from my love of art. The big one is deffinitly my Problem Solving which has always been a strong suit of mine wheater it's figuring out how to fix something or how make something more effeicent. Although it may not be completed I thought that my final year college project was an important one to talk about. My project named Passenger is an mobile application designed for bikers. It uses a phones sensors to graph and chart lateral and longitudinal g-forces in real time to teach bikers about the limits of their vehicle. This is an important one for me as someone raised around bikers I have seen my fair share of crashes and worse because bikes can be deadly if not handled correctly. So the app can show a rider where the limits are by viewing their g-forces compared to a limit.
